Tax Declaration

How to apply

Step 1: Request Slip

Obtain a request slip from the Office of the City Assessor.

Step 2: Fill Up Form

Fill up the necessary forms and proceed to the Cashier’s Counter.

Step 3: Submit Receipt

Submit the receipt to the Office of the City Assessor to finalize your tax declaration process.

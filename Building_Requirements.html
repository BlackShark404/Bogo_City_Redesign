Step 1: Building Plan

Step 2: Building Permit

Provide 4 copies of the approved building permit.

Step 3: Electrical Permit

Submit 3 copies of the electrical permit.

Step 4: Plumbing Permit

Provide 3 copies of the plumbing permit.

Step 5: Specifications

Submit 3 copies of the construction specifications.

Step 6: Bill of Materials

Provide 3 copies of the detailed bill of materials.

Step 1: Title

Provide 2 xerox copies of the land title or related documents (e.g., Deed of Absolute Sale, Affidavit of Consent with original title).

Step 2: Sketch Plan

Submit 2 xerox copies of the sketch plan of the lot.

Step 3: Tax Declaration

Provide 2 xerox copies of the tax declaration.

Step 4: Current Tax Receipt

Submit 2 xerox copies of the current tax receipt.

Step 5: Barangay Clearance

Provide 1 original copy of the Barangay Clearance.

Step 6: Fire Department Clearance

Secure clearance from the Fire Department for final approval.

Step 7: Homeowners Association Clearance

Provide clearance from the Homeowners Association if the structure is built within a developed subdivision.
